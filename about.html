Experience you can trust

Practical knowledge from a lifetime outdoors.

Carey Tree and Garden Services is run by Noel Carey. Noel brings over 35 years’ experience in forestry, timber, machinery and outdoor work, giving every job a practical and safety-focused approach.

This background means customers get more than a quick cut-and-go service. Noel understands trees, timber, machinery, access, ground conditions, storm damage, weather, branches, cuttings and the importance of leaving a site tidy.

The business is built for homeowners, schools, clubs, businesses, property owners and sites that need reliable tree, hedge, garden and grounds maintenance work.

Our approach

Small enough to care, experienced enough to do it right.

Carey Tree and Garden Services is set up to work carefully and professionally, whether the job is a private garden, a hedge line, a school entrance, an estate green area, a car park boundary or a storm-damaged tree.

Jobs are assessed before starting so the price reflects the real work involved: access, time, machinery, chipping, branches, cuttings, risk and clean-up.

What we do

Services for homes, schools, clubs, estates and businesses.

Every job is priced clearly before work begins. Whether it is a hedge, a small tree, a garden clearance or a regular maintenance contract, the aim is always a clean, professional finish.

Tree Work

Small tree cutting, pruning, branch removal and practical tree work for gardens, sites, schools and commercial grounds.

  • Small tree cutting and removal
  • Tree pruning and branch reduction
  • Storm-damaged branches
  • Logs cut and stacked where required

Hedge Cutting

Hedge trimming, shaping and reductions for private homes, boundary lines, schools, estates and commercial properties.

  • Hedge trimming and shaping
  • High and overgrown hedge reductions
  • Seasonal hedge maintenance
  • Cuttings cleared, chipped or removed as agreed

Garden Maintenance

General garden work for customers who want the place brought back under control and kept tidy.

  • Grass cutting and edging
  • Strimming and brushcutting
  • Brambles and overgrown areas
  • Once-off tidy-ups or regular visits

Branch Chipping & Clearances

Branches, hedge cuttings and garden waste can be chipped where suitable, saving space and leaving the job clean.

  • Branches chipped on site where suitable
  • Woodchip can be left for mulch if requested
  • Green waste removed where agreed
  • Useful for hedge work, small trees and clear-outs

Storm Clean-Up

After bad weather, fallen limbs and broken branches can be cut, cleared, chipped or stacked safely.

  • Fallen branches and storm damage
  • Garden and driveway clearances
  • Branches cut and removed
  • Area left tidy and safe

Grounds Maintenance

Reliable grounds maintenance for schools, clubs, car parks, housing estates, apartment blocks and business premises.

  • Grass cutting and strimming
  • Hedges, boundaries and entrances
  • Small tree and branch work
  • Once-off or regular contract work
